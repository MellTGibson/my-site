A little bit about me.

I have a little confession to make, I’m a self-confessed Tech Geek! I love gadgets and all things tech related. A Canadian by birth I currently live in London England where I’ve called home for the past 19 years with my very supportive husband. My background has mainly been in Human Resources within Corporate Finance but I’ve always loved the technical aspects of my job. Any excuse to create something techie I was all over it! Whether it would be an Excel database or a SharePoint site.

My journey into code started when I took a foundations course for Mobile App Development. It was there I was introduced to a bit of HTML and from then on I was hooked on code! I’m now pursuing my passion as a Front End Web Developer utilising my organisational skills and attention to detail to write clean and elegant code. Here I can help my clients and friends create a website that suits their specific needs and creativity.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.
